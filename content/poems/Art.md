Birmingham Art Gallery.

We went straight for the best bit.

Past all the punters

and the Ozzy Osbourne bollocks.

We laughed at them.

We went straight for the best bit.

Right in the back.

Through huge marble doorways and stained glass doors.

There he was.

At long last.

Henri Matisse.
